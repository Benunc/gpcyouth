Grace 101

Greenwood Presbyterian Church

A 12-week Bible study that opens the Word and asks: What is grace? Why do we need it? How do we live in it? For students in 6th–12th grade — and we’d love your help getting them there.

Watch the invite

When & where

  • Every Sunday — Breakfast in the youth room 8:45–9:25 (pastries, fruit, yogurt, and more).
  • Lesson at 9:30 — We open the Bible together. No homework; just show up and ask questions.
  • Starts February 8 — Runs through early May. Ben and Duane teaching.
  • Where — 1414 Calhoun Rd, Greenwood, SC 29649
